Employee Tracking System

Administrator Functions

  • Register new employees
  • Manage company-wide reports
  • Assign vehicle to delivery employees
  • Manage warehouses and inventories
  • View all employee records
  • View all routes and insurance information
  • Access global shipment histories

Supervisor Functions

  • Register new employees
  • Assign vehicle to delivery employees
  • Assign warehouse orders to employees
  • Consult employee information
  • Consult customer information
  • View shipment histories and records
  • View delivery indices and routes
  • Consult package insurance information
  • Consult warehouse-specific inventory

Employee Functions

  • View personal assigned vehicle
  • View assigned delivery routes
  • Access current warehouse orders
  • Check inventory availability in the warehouse
